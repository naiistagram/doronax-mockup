Bronze, Coffee Tables & Wooden Boxes Services

Bronze Sculptures

Cast bronze sculpture pieces for residential and commercial interiors, made to order in a range of scales and finishes.

Each piece is cast and finished by hand, so texture and patina vary slightly by piece — the kind of variation that reads as craft rather than inconsistency.

Coffee Tables

Coffee tables built to order in solid wood, stone, and mixed-material designs, sized and finished to fit the room they're going into.

We work from a set of house designs or a client's own reference, adjusting proportions, material, and finish for the specific space.

Custom Wooden Boxes

Custom wooden boxes for storage, presentation, and gifting, built in a choice of timbers with optional lining, engraving, and hardware.

Sizes and finishes are made to order, from small keepsake boxes through to larger storage and display pieces.
