Interior Design

Full-scheme interior design, managed from first sketch to final styling

Doranax Design and Furniture's interior design team takes on full-scheme residential and commercial projects, managing everything from spatial planning through to final styling under one point of contact. We work with the structure and light of a space first, rather than starting from a mood board, so the finished scheme actually suits how the space is used.

Trade sourcing, procurement, and on-site coordination all sit within the same team that designed the scheme, which means fewer handoffs and fewer compromises between what was drawn and what gets delivered. Every project is treated as bespoke — we don't run a house style that gets applied regardless of the brief.

Our Process

1

Discover

We spend time in the space and with the client to understand how it's actually lived in or used before any design work starts.

2

Define

We set the brief, budget, and scope in writing, so every later decision is measured against an agreed starting point.

3

Design

Spatial planning, material palette, and furnishing scheme are developed together, not as separate sign-offs.

4

Develop

Drawings are refined into a buildable specification, with trade sourcing and procurement running in parallel.

5

Deliver

We manage installation and styling on-site through to handover, so the scheme is finished to the standard it was designed to.
