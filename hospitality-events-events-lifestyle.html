Lifestyle & Leisure Services

Luxury Travel

We plan luxury travel itineraries for private clients, from villa stays to bespoke tours, handled end-to-end by a dedicated travel desk.

Every itinerary is built around the client, not a fixed package — get in touch with dates and a rough brief to start planning.

Gourmet Travels

Our gourmet travel packages combine destination dining with curated itineraries, built for clients who travel for the food.

Itineraries are built around chef's-table bookings, food markets, and regional specialities rather than a generic sightseeing route.

Dance

Sessions run for individuals, private groups, or as part of a wider event booking.

Photography Courses

Small-group sessions cover camera fundamentals through to editing, taught hands-on rather than lecture-style.

Coffee Courses

A relaxed introduction to brewing and tasting, aimed at enthusiasts rather than trade professionals.
