Flooring

Flooring specified as a design decision, not an afterthought

We work across timber, stone, and antique and vintage rugs and carpets, often mixing periods and materials within a single space in the manner of the great English decorators: a 19th-century Sultanabad in a contemporary room, or reclaimed parquet beneath a resolutely modern scheme.

What We Do

Timber Flooring

Solid, engineered, and reclaimed timber flooring specified for grain and how it will wear.

Natural Stone & Tiled Flooring

Stone and tiled flooring matched to the light and use of the room.

Antique & Bespoke Rugs

Antique, vintage, and bespoke rugs and carpets sourced and specified as design pieces.

Underfloor Heating

Underfloor heating specification and integration alongside the flooring choice.

Period Floor Restoration

Restoration of existing period floors, preserving character rather than replacing it.
