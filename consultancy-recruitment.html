IT Sector Recruitment

A specialist arm of our recruitment practice focused on technical hiring — developers, infrastructure, product, and IT leadership roles — where a generalist search process usually falls short.

We screen for technical depth as well as fit, working with hiring managers to define the actual skill bar for a role rather than recycling a generic job spec. Candidates are assessed by people who understand the stack they'd be working in.

Coverage spans permanent, contract, and interim technical hires, from individual contributor roles through to CTO and Head of Engineering searches.
