Export/Import

Guiding businesses through every stage of international trade

Doranax Consultancy's export/import desk guides businesses through each step of their international trade journey — from initial market assessment through to goods clearing customs at the other end. We handle the parts of cross-border trade that stall most businesses: regulation, documentation, and the relationships needed to keep freight moving.

Our consultancy covers trade regulations, customs procedures, tariffs, and trade agreements specific to each market, alongside the global logistics needed to get goods from origin to destination reliably. We handle documentation and help plan trade finance strategies so cash flow isn't the thing that derails an otherwise sound deal.

Because we work directly with in-market representatives rather than a single head-office desk, our advice reflects what's actually happening on the ground in each country, not a generic international-trade playbook.
