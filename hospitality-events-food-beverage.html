Coffee and Barista Courses

Our barista training programme covers espresso extraction, milk texturing, and bar service standards, run by working baristas for staff who need to hit a professional standard fast.

Courses are run for individual trainees or as a full staff onboarding programme for a new venue.

Coffee Beans

We source and roast coffee beans for venues and retail, offering a range of blends and single origins with consistent supply for busy service.

Wholesale and retail-bagged supply are both available, with consistent roast profiles maintained batch to batch.

Cake

Our bakery produces cakes for venues, events, and retail, from everyday counter stock to bespoke celebration pieces made to order.

Every cake is baked from scratch to order, with a consultation up front to lock in flavour, size, and design before anything goes in the oven. We cover birthday and celebration designs alongside occasion-specific work for weddings, corporate events, and baby showers.

Delivery and collection are both available, timed around your event so the cake arrives in the condition it left the kitchen in.

Burger

Our burger concept keeps things simple — a small, tightly run menu built around a smash-style patty, quality ingredients, and fast, precise cooking.

No sprawling menu, no gimmicks — just a format we've deliberately kept simple so it can be executed consistently, service after service, for customers who want something good and quick rather than a long wait for a novelty burger.
