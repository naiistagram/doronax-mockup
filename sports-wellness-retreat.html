Retreat Services

Morocco

Our Morocco retreat combines desert and riad settings with daily movement and rest, run in small groups for a genuinely restorative pace.

Days are built around morning practice, guided rest, and communal meals, with the desert and riad settings doing the rest of the work.

Turkiye

Our Turkiye retreat pairs coastal and Cappadocia settings with a programme of movement, rest, and nutrition in a small-group format.

The itinerary moves between coast and Cappadocia across the retreat, balancing structured sessions with genuine unstructured downtime.
